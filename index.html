THE IACG
WORLD GOLF CHAMPIONSHIP

Established 2026

The IACG WGC is a storied and exceedingly rare championship awarded to the winner of a head-to-head game of golf between members of the I.A.C.G.

Like all legendary sporting institutions, nobody really knows how it started, nor why it continues.

The title is worth absolutely nothing, won't get you a free pint, and certainly doesn't prove you're a particularly good golfer.

Friends, family and colleagues are likely to think less of you should you ever mention it.

...and yet it's all you can think about.
